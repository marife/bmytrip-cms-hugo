Itinerary

We will depart from Hotel in Puerto Natales or Bmytrip Meeting point at 07:00 am heading to Torres del Paine National Park, direct transfer through the mythical Route 9 to Estancia Cerro Paine, starting point of our Experience. We will start our trekking around 09:00 hrs, after a crompehensive lecture by our guide. Afterwards we will take the trail towards Ascencio Valley and after 10 minutes walking, we will cross an old suspension bridge that crosses the Ascencio River, about 150 meters ahead, a bifurcation appears: our destiny ... Base Torres.

Then follows a zigzag of marked slope and scarce vegetation, we will see the windy and spectacular Ascencio Valley. After almost an hour of walking; We will arrive at the El Chileno Camping on the opposite bank of the Ascencio River. Then begins our second chapter of the trekking, with a complete change of vegetation. Up to this point it corresponds to the Pre-Andean bushes ""Matorral"" and that from then on it will completely change, since it will go into an old Lenga forest where we will ascend gently until we reach the moraine of the Torres Glacier (at 600 meters above sea level).

When leaving the forest we will have one last climb, walking for 45 minutes uphill. The trail will take us to the base Torres (at 870 masl), the 3 granite giants (Central, Monzino, Dagostini) and Peineta and Nido de Cóndores hills. After resting a while, we will have lunch in this extraordinary and unique scenic context, unique on the planet.

We begin our return along the same path until we reach the starting point, where our driver will be waiting to begin our return to Puerto Natales or BMYTRIP meeting point.
